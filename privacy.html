Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 July 2026

Creator Radar (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store information when you use our website at https://creatorradar.co.uk and any related services (collectively, the “Service”).

1. Information We Collect

When you use the Service, we may collect:

  • Your email address, if you create an account.
  • Basic TikTok profile information (open ID, display name, avatar) when you authorise Login Kit.
  • Content you generate or queue for posting, such as captions, hooks, and hashtags.
  • Anonymous usage analytics to improve the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide content research and trend suggestions.
  • Authenticate you and connect your TikTok account.
  • Upload videos to your TikTok account as drafts, only when you explicitly choose to do so.
  • Improve and secure the Service.

3. TikTok Data

We only request the minimum permissions needed: basic profile info and video upload access. We never store your TikTok password. You can revoke access at any time in your TikTok app settings.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with service providers necessary to operate the Service, such as Supabase for database hosting and Vercel for hosting.

5. Data Retention

We keep your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and data by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.

7. Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at support@creatorradar.co.uk.
